Why ProjectOS

Not another app. An OS layer.

Your tools already exist. ProjectOS makes them intelligent — aware of each other, aware of your work.

🧠

Cross-tool intelligence

ProjectOS reads context from every connected tool simultaneously. Ask one question — get answers synthesized from GitHub, Jira, Slack, IDE, and Gmail at once. No context-switching, ever.

⚡

Actions, not answers

ProjectOS doesn't just tell you what to do — it does it. Assign reviewers, update tickets, send Slack summaries. Intent becomes action across your stack.

🔗

Connected context

A commit, a Jira ticket, a Slack thread, an email — all one unit of work. ProjectOS links them automatically, refreshing every hour so your context is always current.

🛡️

Built for enterprise

SOC 2 compliant. Zero data retention mode. Deploy on your own infra. Your work data stays yours — always.
